Site Purpose

The website provides rafting enthusiasts with trip information, safety tips, and booking options. It serves as a hub for adventure seekers to explore guided rafting trips, view river conditions, and connect with experienced rafting guides.

Scenarios

  • Which rafting trips are best for beginners and families?
  • How can I book a trip and what equipment is provided?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used across the website:

  • Primary Color (#1E90FF - Dodger Blue): Used for headings, navigation bar, and accents.
  • Secondary Color (#FFD700 - Golden Yellow): Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights.

Typography

The site will use the following fonts:

  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for body text for readability.
  • Merriweather (Serif): Used for headings to give a professional and adventurous look.
